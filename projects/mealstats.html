Definition

What?

Mealstats is an app for the apple watch, designed to help count calories and other nutritional stats.

How?

It allows the users to record each of their meals and adds the value of the desired nutrient to a total value based on recommended daily and weekly intake. The personal information of the user that is required, as well as the nutrients to be recorded would be configured externally.

Who?

The intended users are adults between 25 to 45 years old, who worry about their health and struggle to keep a healthy diet.

Why?

Dietary medical concerns are widespread nowadays due to processed foods and limited time to prepare quality meals. The app is designed for people to make fast dietary desicions based on the information they find most relevant.
